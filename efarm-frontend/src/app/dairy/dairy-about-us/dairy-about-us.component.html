Our Story

Peter's Dairy was founded in 1980 by Peter, a third-generation dairy farmer. Since then, we've been committed to providing the highest quality milk products to our customers. We believe in sustainable farming practices and treating our cows with the utmost care and respect.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide the freshest, highest quality milk products to our customers while practicing sustainable and ethical farming practices. We believe that happy cows produce better milk, and we strive to make sure our cows are well-cared for and content.
